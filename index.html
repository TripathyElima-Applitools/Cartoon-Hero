About Tom and Jerry

My 4-year-old son is absolutely convinced that Tom and Jerry are not just cartoon characters but real, flesh-and-fur citizens of the USA. According to his detailed (and suspiciously well-thought-out) theory, they live in a sprawling suburban mansion, complete with a garage full of tiny sports cars for their high-speed chases. He’s pretty sure they hang out in a neighborhood where Bugs Bunny mows the lawn, Scooby-Doo hosts BBQs, and Daffy Duck argues over HOA fees. Honestly, if this place exists, I might just pack up and move there—at least their drama sounds more entertaining than ours!
